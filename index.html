About Maharashtra

The Land of Warriors & Wisdom

Maharashtra is India's second-largest state by area and third-largest by population. Bordered by the Arabian Sea to the west, it is home to Mumbai — India's financial capital — and Pune, its cultural twin. The state gave birth to Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj's legendary Maratha Empire, and its forts stand as testament to an indomitable spirit.

From the sun-drenched Konkan coast to the misty Sahyadri hills, from the bustling streets of Nagpur to the pilgrimage towns of Pandharpur and Shirdi — Maharashtra contains multitudes.
